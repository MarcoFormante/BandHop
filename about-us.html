Ou itemprop="name"r History

Bandhop is a music association founded in London in 2015 by a group of passionate music lovers who wanted to create a platform that would connect emerging bands with national festival and local venues. Over the years, it has grown into a big community of artists and venue owners where they share the love of the music.
Today, we are the most important platform for all emerging bands and artists and we continue to help musicians achieve their goals and realize their dreams.

Bandhop Team

At Bandhop we believe that our success is based on the collective efforts of our talented and dedicated team member. Each individual brings unique skills and perspectives to the table, allowing us to provide the best possible experience for our users.

Our itemprop="name" Mission

At Bandhop , our mission is to empower emerging artists and revolutionize the way live music event are organized. Our platform provides a unique opportunity for musicians to connect with venues and create unforgettable experiences for their audiences.
Join us in our mission to make live music accessible to all!
